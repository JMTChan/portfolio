About

I build the kind of websites that quietly do their job well — fast, responsive, and built with intention.

For the past 3+ years I've been designing and developing custom websites using Wix Classic, Wix Studio, and Velo — from business sites and CMS-driven content platforms to legal practice websites and political campaign pages.

More recently I've moved into AI-assisted, full-stack web development, working with Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, Prisma, and PostgreSQL — using tools like Claude Code to ship complex features faster without giving up on craft.

I'm based in Davao City, Philippines, and work with clients across the US and worldwide. Most of my work comes from returning clients — which I take as the best signal I'm doing something right.

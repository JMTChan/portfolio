About

Websites that quietly do their job well

I build the kind of websites that quietly do their job well — fast, responsive, and built with intention rather than decoration.

For 3+ years I've designed and developed custom sites using Wix Classic, Wix Studio, and Velo — from business and CMS-driven platforms to legal practice sites and political campaign pages.

More recently I've moved into AI-assisted, full-stack development with Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, Prisma, and PostgreSQL — using tools like Claude Code to ship complex features faster without giving up on craft. Most of my work comes from returning clients, which I take as the best signal I'm doing something right.
